Play Chess with a friend


To play with a friend, enter a room name. If the room does not exist, it will be created. If the room exists, you will join it.





Other players can join your room by inputting its name.

How to play Chess


In Chess, the aim is to checkmate your opponent's king. If playing against a friend, just type your name and the room name and click play.


On your turn, move a piece by clicking the piece and clicking a valid square or by dragging the piece to a valid square. If your piece can capture, a circle will appear around the opponent's piece. To castle, drag the king over the rook or click the square next to the rook.


Once a player has their opponent's king in checkmate, the winner will get 1 point and the game will end. If the game ends by stalemate both players will get 1 point. To rematch your opponent, just click 'Rematch' and a rematch will start for both players. To quit, just click 'Home' on the rematch menu or the house icon in the top left corner.
