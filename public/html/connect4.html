How to play Connect 4


In Connect 4, the aim is to get 4 tiles in a row. If playing against a friend, just type your name and the room name and click play.


On your turn, you play a tile by clicking in a column that isn't full. If a column is full, you cannot play pieces in that column. To get 4 in a row, tiles can be in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.


Once a player has 4 tiles in a row, the winner will get 1 point and the game will end. If the game ends by a draw both players will get 1 point. To rematch your opponent, just click 'Rematch' and a rematch will start for both players. To quit, just click 'Home' on the rematch menu or the house icon in the top left corner.
